Description

An interactive data visualization to highlight the results of the study Hydrologic Budget and Conditions of Permian, Pennsylvanian, and Mississippian aquifers in the Appalachian Plateaus Physiographic Province. The report presents a water budget for the entire Plateaus region. State agencies do not consider the PMAS aquifers as one cohesive hydrologic system. Resource managers, specifically those managing hydrologic dependent ecosystems (i.e., fisheries), function at the watershed scale. A watershed-based presentation of the data would be a beneficial means to disseminate the results of the PMAS water budget report, provide a gateway to new datasets, and enhance the report readership.

This Info Sheet provide a summary of the Appalachian Plateaus Groundwater Availability.
